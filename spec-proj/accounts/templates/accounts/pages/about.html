Do not stay up late. (Discipline yourself.) Protect your eyes from light to reduce stress and strain on the optic nerve. The human eye can only recognize 10 percent of the visible light spectrum, so when you're in a dark environment, try to squint a little. Your retina can be damaged by excessive exposure to light, so worrying about damage may seem unnecessary.

Wear a pair of glasses. Being nearsighted or farsighted means your eyes aren't focusing correctly on things to see them clearly in front of you. Over time, this problem can get worse. Wearing glasses helps keep your eyes healthy and able to focus.
